Farm Activities & Walks

Embrace the magic of each season with our special themed walks!

Enjoy the beauty of spring wildflowers, the vibrant colors of fall foliage, or the winter serenity of a snow-covered landscape, making every visit unique and memorable.

Stroll along our peaceful nature trails and discover the beauty of local flora and fauna. With birdwatching spots and peaceful rest areas, it’s the perfect opportunity to unwind, spot wildlife, and enjoy the great outdoors.

Animal Zoo & Farm

Get up close and personal with our friendly farm animals! Feed the goats, sheep, and chickens, or visit our charming petting zoo where you can cuddle cute bunnies and baby lambs.

It’s a hands-on experience that’s perfect for families and animal lovers!

This experience is perfect for kids and animal lovers alike—an unforgettable way to connect with our furry, feathered friends.

Pick-Your-Own Harvest

Experience the joy of harvesting fresh, seasonal produce right from the farm! Pick juicy strawberries in summer, pumpkins in fall, or apples in the orchard—take home the freshest farm-grown treats.

When you arrive, you’ll be given a basket or container, and our friendly staff will direct you to the areas where the produce is ready to be picked. The farm will have clearly marked rows or patches for each type of crop.

Hiking & Walks

Find peace and tranquility on the Mindful Trail, designed for reflection and relaxation.

With calming views, designated quiet zones, and spots for meditation or deep breathing exercises, this walk is a retreat for those looking to connect with nature and clear their minds.

For stunning panoramic views choose Scenic Overlook Trail. It leads you up a gentle incline to a hilltop where you can see the entire farm and surrounding landscape.

It’s a fantastic spot for a photographies, with sweeping views of fields, forests, and distant hills.
